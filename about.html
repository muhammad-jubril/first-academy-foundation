Who We Are

Welcome to First Academy Foundation

First Academy Foundation Nursery, Primary & Secondary School is dedicated to nurturing young minds through quality education, strong moral values, creativity and academic excellence.

We believe every child has the potential to succeed when provided with the right environment, dedicated teachers and modern learning facilities.

  • Qualified, dedicated teaching staff
  • Modern ICT and science facilities
  • Safe, nurturing school community

Our Mission

To provide every child with quality education, excellent character and lifelong values that prepare them to become responsible leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be a leading school known for academic excellence, strong values and the safe, caring environment in which every learner grows.

Our Promise

A supportive, secure environment where every learner can grow academically, socially and morally, guided by dedicated teachers.
