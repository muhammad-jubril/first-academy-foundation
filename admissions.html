How It Works

Our Enrollment Process

A simple four-step process to join the First Academy Foundation family.

  1. 1

    Inquire

    Reach out by phone or the contact form to ask about openings and requirements.

  2. 2

    Visit & Assess

    Tour the school and complete a simple placement conversation with our staff.

  3. 3

    Submit Documents

    Provide the required registration documents to confirm your child's place.

  4. 4

    Welcome Aboard

    Your child resumes and joins the First Academy Foundation family.

What You'll Generally Need

  • Your child's birth certificate or age verification
  • Previous school report or transfer letter (if applicable)
  • Immunization/health record
  • Passport photographs
  • Completed registration form (available at the school office)

Exact requirements can vary by class level — contact us and we'll send the full checklist for your child's year group.
